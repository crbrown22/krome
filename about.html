Christopher Brown

NSCA-CSCS, USAW-L1, EXOS-XPS, EXOS-XFS
OWNER / CEO / COACH

Coach Brown graduated from Midwest City High School where he was an All-District utility baseball player. Graduating in 2002, he recieved a baseball scholarship to Rose State College where he played 2 years as the starting centerfielder for the Raiders. Once graduating from RSC, Brown accepted a scholarship to play at Saint Gregory's University in Shawnee, Oklahoma earning a Gold Glove Award and a 2nd Team All Conference in the Sooner Athletic Conference.

    Education

  • A.S Personal Training & Fitness Management-Rose State College
  • B.S Business Management-Saint Gregory's University
  • M.A Teaching and Leadership-Southern Nazerene University

    Experience

  • Owner / Trainer - KROME SPORTS PERFORMANCE (2018-Present)
